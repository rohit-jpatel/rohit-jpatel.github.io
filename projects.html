  • Innovative Machine Design:

    • Advanced Tooling: Designed custom drill bits optimized for various materials, incorporating high-performance coatings to manage wear and extend tool life. The choice of materials and design was crucial for maintaining precision and durability.
  • Automation Mastery:
    • Seamless Integration: Integrated state-of-the-art automation with servo motors and programmable logic controllers (PLCs). This setup allowed for precise control over drilling operations, ensuring both heads worked in perfect harmony.

  • Precision and Quality Control:
    • Custom Fixtures: Engineered bespoke fixtures with sophisticated clamping mechanisms to secure components during drilling. These fixtures were designed to ensure minimal movement and maximum precision, addressing challenges specific to delicate materials.
