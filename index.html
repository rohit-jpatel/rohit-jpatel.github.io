Courses Enrolled

Listed the courses enrolled for Fall 2021 Semester.

CS541 - Database Systems

Techniques and methodologies for the design and implementation of data-based information systems. Topics include database design, modeling and management, database query design and implementation languages, physical structures and algorithms for storage and access, data mining models, complex event processing, control of data access, security and integrity, compliance with laws and regulations for databases. Prerequisite: Discrete Structures.

CS528 - Special Topics in Systems & Architectures - Quantum Computing

Quantum information and computation exploits quantum mechanical rules to process information. Asa new branch of interdisciplinary science, it has both fundamental and technological implications. This course is designed to introduce graduate computer science students (or advanced undergraduate students) to the fundamentals of quantum computing and its applications.
