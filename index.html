Rohit Patel

Rohit is pursuing a Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering at Clemson University, specializing in Design and Manufacturing. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University (2018), where his research on advanced machining techniques led to publications on stainless-steel machining challenges and titanium alloy milling optimization. With over six years of professional experience, Rohit has held leadership roles in companies like Gabriel India Ltd., Orbital Systems, and Sukhdhan Automation Pvt. Ltd. As a Senior Manufacturing Engineer at Gabriel India Ltd., he improved automotive production processes, increasing machining efficiency by 25% and reducing costs by over $150,000 annually. Rohit’s expertise includes Six Sigma, DFMEA, PFMEA, APQP methodologies, and proficiency in design tools like SolidWorks, SOLIDEDGE, and ANSYS. Rohit has earned accolades such as the Best Paper Award at SPICON 2022 for his work in manufacturing. His commitment to innovation drives his passion for creating efficient, sustainable engineering solutions.
