Advanced Design Methodologies

Focuses on fostering creativity and decision-making in mechanical design. The course provides an in-depth study of design processes and tools, covering topics such as quality function deployment, concurrent design, systemic design, robust design, design for assembly, and axiomatic design.

Grade: B

Engineering Optimization

Explores optimization techniques in engineering design. Topics include nonlinear and linear optimization, static and dynamic problems, and constrained and unconstrained formulations. The course covers practical applications like structural and multiobjective optimization, along with advanced methods such as genetic algorithms and simulated annealing.

Grade: A

Introduction to Finite Element Analysis

Provides an introduction to the finite element method and its applications in heat transfer, fluid flow, and solid mechanics. The course covers transient analysis, strategies for using finite elements, and introduces solid modeling, finite element modeling, and analysis using commercial software tools.

Grade: A
